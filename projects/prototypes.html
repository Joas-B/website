Solo project - Godot Engine - Development period: Sep. 2021 - May 2022
-> Link to itch.io page
This was a solo project, and as such I created and put together every element of the game, with the exception of the creation of the audio assets.
  • Planning and direction of the game, using emotion-based design to create a specific emotional experience for players.
  • Designed, programmed and iterated on the "rhythmic" walking mechanic that enhances the sense of physical exploration.
  • Designed the level, and modelled and implemented the environment.
  • Created a custom artstyle for the game by programming shaders.
  • Wrote dialogue for various characters.
About the Project
This was my Honours project for university, meaning I spent most of a school year working on the design, proposal, development, and a dissertation.
The focus of the game was to recreate the experience of walking a pilgrimage. The primary was a unique, rhytmic walking mechanic that made players more immersed in the experience, and felt more physical. I create a large, stylistic environment that immerses players in the atmosphere as they explore it. To add on to this, I included several characters also on a pilgrimage, who all have their own perspective and experience with it. This created a sense of introspection and human connection, which would make players consider their own perspective in it.
As such, the final result was a meditative, relaxing, atmospheric, and introspective experience for players, and I received very positive feedback on the game.
